Privacy Policy – Color Extractor

Effective Date: January 2026

Color Extractor (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information Collection

Color Extractor does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The App does not require user registration and does not request your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information.

2. Camera and Photo Library Access

The App may request access to your device camera or photo library solely for the purpose of allowing you to select or capture an image in order to extract color values (HEX and RGB codes).

All image processing occurs locally on your device. Images are not uploaded to any server and are not stored by the App after processing.

3. Data Processing

Color values are calculated directly on the device using local processing. No image data or extracted color data is transmitted outside your device.

4. Third-Party Services

This version of the App does not use third-party analytics services, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or external APIs.

5. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no remote storage of user information. All operations are performed locally on the user’s device.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect future changes in the App. Any updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
